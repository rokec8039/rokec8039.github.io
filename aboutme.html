ABOUT GUY FIERI

Things he enjoys doing

Guy Fieri enjoys many things. One thing he enjoys is food. He loves anything related to food. Another thing Guy Fieri likes is eating. His favorite activity is eating. Sometimes, Guy eats for many hours, sometimes days, at a time. He also enjoys cooking. He owns his own restaurant on the Las Vegas strip, reknowned worldwide for his flavorful recipes. Guy Fieri's main job is being the host of food related television shows on Food Network. Some of his most popular shows are "Diners, Drive-Ins and Dives", "Guy's Grocery Games", and "Rachael vs. Guy: Celebrity Cook-Off".
